Introduction

Welcome to Tipd. We're committed to being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it's used when you use our Dallas discovery app.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Email address — required to create your account
  • Username — optional, chosen by you
  • Full name — optional
  • Reviews and likes you post within the app

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — we track which features, places, and content you tap or view to personalize your experience
  • Basic device information for troubleshooting and performance purposes

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your reviews and liked places in the app
  • Personalize your experience based on your in-app activity
  • Improve the app's features and performance
  • Communicate with you about your account or policy updates
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the app

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service provider:

  • Supabase — our backend database and infrastructure provider. Your data is stored on Supabase's servers under appropriate data protection terms. Learn more at supabase.com/privacy.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and personal data
  • Opt-out — contact us to opt out of certain data uses

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at app.tipd@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Tipd is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date above or through an in-app notification. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
